Got something big on the horizon you'd have to spend money on? Are you itching to buy something or want to save up to go on a trip abroad? Big expense planner can help you!

Just put in the amount you'd need to save and either the number of days you wish to recover by or the cut in your budget you're willing to take and change your budget accordingly.

You'll be saving without even realizing on a daily basis for your big expense!
